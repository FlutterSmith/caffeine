Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer WiFi?

Yes! We offer free high-speed WiFi to all customers. The password is available on your receipt or ask any staff member.

Can I host events at Caffeine?

Absolutely! We have space available for private events, meetings, and gatherings. Contact us to discuss your needs and availability.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we provide catering for corporate events, meetings, and special occasions. Choose from our coffee service packages and food platters.

Are there vegan and gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a variety of plant-based milk alternatives and several vegan and gluten-free food options. Check our menu for details.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes, gift cards are available in-store and online. They make perfect gifts for coffee lovers and never expire.

Can I buy your coffee beans?

Absolutely! We sell whole bean and ground coffee in-store. We also offer online ordering with nationwide shipping.
