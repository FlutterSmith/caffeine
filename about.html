Our Story

Where passion meets perfection in every cup

The Beginning

Founded in 2015, Caffeine began with a simple dream: to create a space where exceptional coffee meets genuine community connection.

Our founder, Maria Santos, grew up on a small coffee farm in Colombia, where she learned that great coffee starts with respectâ€”respect for the land, the farmers, and the craft. After years of traveling the world and studying coffee culture in Italy, Ethiopia, and beyond, she returned with a vision to bring authentic, ethically-sourced coffee to our neighborhood.

What started as a small corner shop with just three tables has grown into a beloved community hub, but our core values remain unchanged: quality, sustainability, and connection.

Our Mission & Values

Sustainability

We source 100% of our beans from certified organic and fair-trade farms. Every purchase supports sustainable farming practices and ensures fair wages for coffee farmers worldwide.

Quality

From bean selection to the final pour, we never compromise on quality. Our master roasters carefully craft each blend, and our baristas are trained to deliver perfection in every cup.

Community

More than a coffee shop, we're a gathering place. We host local artists, support community events, and create a welcoming space where everyone belongs.

Passion

Every member of our team shares a genuine love for coffee and people. This passion drives us to continuously improve and to create memorable experiences for every guest.

Ethical Sourcing

We believe great coffee should benefit everyone involved in its journey.

  • Direct relationships with coffee farmers in 12 countries
  • Fair Trade and Rainforest Alliance certified beans
  • Support for sustainable farming practices
  • Regular visits to origin farms to ensure quality and ethics
  • Transparent pricing that benefits producers

Our commitment to ethical sourcing means you can enjoy your coffee knowing it was produced with respect for people and planet.

Giving Back

We believe in being an active, positive force in our community. Through our "Coffee for a Cause" program, 5% of every purchase goes to local education initiatives. We provide free meeting space for community groups, showcase local artists' work, and partner with neighborhood organizations to create positive change.

Our commitment extends globally too. We've funded clean water projects in coffee-growing regions, supported educational programs for farmers' children, and invested in sustainable agriculture research.
